<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><strong><br/></strong></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><strong><span style="; ">슬슬 이 작가의 테루모코가</span></strong></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><strong><span style="; ">정리가 되기 시작한다</span></strong></p>

<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; ">뭐랄까</span></b></p>

<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; ">모코가 테루요를 사랑-&gt; 냄새를 맡음 -&gt; 킁킁 -&gt; 고백 -&gt; 오해 -&gt; 폭발엔딩</span></b></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; ">음?</span><br/><span style="; ">아닌가?</span></b></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><br/></b></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; ">어쨌든 확실한건</span></b></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; ">모코가 부끄러워하는 모습이</span></b></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; ">잔뜩 나온다는 것 정도</span></b></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; "><br/></span></b></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; ">요즘들어서 사고력 감퇴가 급격히 심각해졌기에</span></b></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; ">글이 써지는건지 뭔지...</span></b></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; "><br/></span></b></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; ">위의 말들은 </span></b></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; font-family: 돋움, dotum, verdana, sans-serif; text-align: center;"><b><span style="; ">이해못하시는게 정상입니다</span></b></p>
